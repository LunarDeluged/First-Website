Coffee

An iced coffee recipe

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • ½ cup Gevalia® Cold Brew Concentrate Caramel
  • Coffee creamer
  • Coconut flavoured syrup
  • Whipped topping
  • Coconut flakes
  • Caramel ice crem topping

Directions

  1. Fill a large glass with ice cubes. Add cold brew concentrate, creamer, and coconut syrup; stir well.
  2. Garnish with whipped topping, coconut flakes, and/or caramel syrup as desired.
